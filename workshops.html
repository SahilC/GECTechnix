SIXTHSENSE BOTZ

India’s first “SixthSense Botz” vision robotics workshop designed by team ARK. In this workshop, we have tried to interface digital world with physical world. We all sit in front of computer entire day for our daily activity and one hand is busy clicking the things on the computer, then we felt to design something that can interface the real human with the computer. We humans can see the things around us, differentiate between colors then why can’t machine? And that thought leads us to design “sixthsenseBotz” In this two day workshop, using a camera, the robot will be able to tell what the robot is sensing.

COURSE CONTENT

For processing of the images, we use a tool called as MATLAB, which is widely used in industry.In this workshop, the applications that will be taught to the participants are as mentioned below:-

  1. Color Recognition Application
  2. Ball following robot
  3. Gesture controlled robot.
  4. Gesture controlled windows media player.
  5. Gesture controlled mouse pointer

DURATION

We conduct workshops on 2 consecutive days, each day 8 hours session so in total 16 hours properly divided into theory and hands on sessions.
