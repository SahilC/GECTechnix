WORKSHOPS

Do you want to cope up with state of the art technologies and want an enjoyable and enriching experience at the same time? Or maybe interact with students having the same interests and give yourself a boost in the subject? If yes, then Technix workshops are tailor-made for you. Applying the time-tested principle of "Do while you learn", workshops promise to be an enjoyable yet enriching experience to the participants, with significant interaction amongst peers sharing the same interests.
