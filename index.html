F.A.Q.

What is Technix?
Technix is the annual, national level technical festival conducted by the
Computer Science department of the Goa College of Engineering (GEC).
Students compete in over 20 events, with a prize money of over Rs. 2 lakhs,
Technix now stands as one of the biggest celebrations of
technology in India, where the brightest minds in the country gather to
compete and present their ideas

What is CURSOR?
CURSOR is the Computer Engineering Student's Organization.It came into existence in 1995 and Since has been responsible for organizing various events relating primarily to the field of Information Technology, to benefit the students of, not just Computer Science and Engineering, but also Electronics and Telecommunication and Information technology.

What is the significance of the seven in Technix 7?
This is the seventh consecutive year in which, the Goa College of Engineering, in association with CURSOR, will host This event.

Who can participate in Technix 7?
Anyone with an urge to learn, and be awesome are welcome to participate in Technix 7!

What do I need to carry to the event?
You should carry an Identification card to the event. Before participating in any events, your registration ID must be provided.You will get a registration ID, after you register for the Event.

How to Reach Our College?
